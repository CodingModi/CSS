Rainy Season

The Beauty of Rain

The rainy season is one of the most beautiful seasons of the year. It brings cool weather, fresh air, and greenery everywhere. Rain fills rivers, lakes, and ponds, making nature lively and refreshing.

Why We Love the Rainy Season

  • Pleasant and cool weather.
  • Green trees and colorful flowers.
  • Beautiful rainbows after rain.
  • Hot tea and delicious snacks.
  • Helps farmers grow crops.

"Rain is nature's way of washing the world clean and bringing new life."
